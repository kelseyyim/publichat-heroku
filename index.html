What is Publichat?

Publichat is a space to have anonymous conversations. This easy-to-use chat room is a perfect place to quickly strike up a conversation amongst friends, family, or other anonymous users! With no account creation necessary, we want Publichat to be free and simple for all to use. Whenever you are ready to begin chatting, click the start chatting button above and have fun!
